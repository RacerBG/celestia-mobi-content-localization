WASP-121, also known as TOI-495 and Gaia-TROI-121, is an F-type main sequence star about 887.19 light-years away in the constellation Puppis. A very large jovian planet completes a 1.28-day orbit around its star. Both objects will receive formal names along with 20 other systems upon the conclusion of the third NameExoWorlds campaign on April 2023
