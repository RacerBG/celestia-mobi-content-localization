Exploration Flight Test-1 or EFT-1 was the maiden flight of NASA's Orion spacecraft. It was launched on 2014-Dec-5, 12:05 UTC by a Delta IV Heavy rocket from which it orbited Earth twice before testing its heat shield upon reentry about four hours into flight

NOTES:

  • EFT-1 can only be seen on 2014-Dec-5 from 12:05 to 16:29 UTC
  • The XYZV trajectory used is only about less than 90% historically accurate due to lack of actual state vector data publicly available in sites such as JPL HORIZONS
