Inspiration4 (stylized as Inspiratiâ‘£n) was a human orbital spaceflight operated by SpaceX on behalf of Shift4 Payments CEO Jared Isaacman. The mission launched the Crew Dragon Resilience on 2021-Sept-16 on a three-day orbital mission. The mission successfully completed the first orbital spaceflight with only private citizens aboard and was part of a charitable effort on behalf of St. Jude's in Memphis, Tennessee. The crew consisted of Jared Isaacman, Hayley Arceneaux, Chris Sembroski, and Dr. Sian Proctor
