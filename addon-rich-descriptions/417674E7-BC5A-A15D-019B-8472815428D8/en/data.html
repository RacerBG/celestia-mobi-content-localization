WASP-107, aka K2-235 and TOI-1905, is a K-type main sequence star located about 211.6 lightyears from the solar system. The star is orbited by two large gas giants, one with a very low density (b= 10.3 Re, 30.5 Me | c= 115 Me). WASP-107 b is known to have a near-polar retrograde orbit around its host. It has also been observed to have a helium trail behind it.
