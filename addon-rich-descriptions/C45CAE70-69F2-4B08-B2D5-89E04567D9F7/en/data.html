YSES 1, also known as TYC 8998-760-1, is a young K-type pre-main sequence star about 309.45 light-years away in the constellation Musca. Estimated to be around 17 million years old, the star is the first known multiplanetary system discovered via direct imaging, where two super-jovian planets were spotted orbiting about 162 and 320 AU away from their host
