TOI-715 is a red dwarf located about 138.42 lightyears from the solar system. A single potentially rocky super-Earth (b= 1.55 Re) orbits well within the star's habitable zone, no mass measurements of this planet have been taken yet. There is also a candidate second planet, which has its own dedicated addon here.
