This addon shows a selection of exo-planets compared with the planets of our solar system (Pluto included) and some of their moons around the star 18 Scorpii. HD 149026 b(the hottest known exo-planet) was modified to give its suspected appearance,and Eps Eri c and d, and Lalande 21185 b and c were included despite the fact that they have not been confirmed. (Note: Addon has not been updated since 2011.)
