Psi1 Draconis, also known as Dziban, is a triple star system consisting of an F-type subgiant, a K-type main sequence star, and a Sun-like star about 69.87 light-years away in the constellation Draco. The secondary star (component B) has one (or two) superjovian planets discovered, but as of 2022 only the innermost planet's existence is confirmed
