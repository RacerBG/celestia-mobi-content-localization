GOES-N is the third-generation series of weather satellites under NASA/NOAA's Geostationary Operational Environmental Satellites program (1975-Present). Three satellites of this series (designated as 13, 14, and 15) are launched from 2006-2010. They are planned to be replaced by the fourth-generation GOES-R
