WD 0806-661, also known as GJ 3483, is a white dwarf star about 62.8 light-years away in the constellation Volans. In 2011, a massive superjovian planet or a low-end brown dwarf was discovered by the Spitzer Space Telescope orbiting at a distance of 2,500 AU from its host star. It is one of the candidate systems to receive formal names as part of the third NameExoWorlds campaign in 2022
