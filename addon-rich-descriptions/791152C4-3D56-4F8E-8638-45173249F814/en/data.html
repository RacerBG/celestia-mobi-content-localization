SPECULOOS-2, also known as LP 890-9 and TOI-4306, is a red dwarf star about 105.44 light-years away in the constellation Eridanus. In 2022, two large rocky planets were initially detected by TESS and their existence were later confirmed by the SPECULOOS observatories in Chile. The outermost planet is located in the star's habitable zone, and is one of the favourable targets for atmospheric characterization using the JWST
