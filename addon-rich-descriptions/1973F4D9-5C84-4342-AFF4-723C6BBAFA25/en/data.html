These are a series of 11 CELX scripts that will mark all stars that are known to harbor at least one (confirmed) exoplanet since 1988.

Known planetary systems located in Kepler's original Field-of-View, 2009-2013

List of CELX scripts included. :
  • Mark All Exoplanets
  • Mark Astrometry
  • Mark CoRoT
  • Mark Direct Imaging
  • Mark Doppler Spectroscopy
  • Mark Gaia
  • Mark Gravitational Microlensing
  • Mark Kepler
  • Mark Kepler-K2
  • Mark TESS
  • Mark Transit
