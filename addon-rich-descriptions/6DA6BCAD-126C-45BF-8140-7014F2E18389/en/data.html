A fictional Cassini-type spacecraft launched in June 18, 2019 to study Uranus and its moons from 2030-2033, where it briefly orbited Oberon from 2031-2032. It is the fifth of eight missions as part of the Toutatis Program (2018-2047), although chronologically it was the seventh launched (after Toutatis-7, 3, 6 and 4)
