AFGL 4106, also known as HD 302821, is a distant binary star system composed of a red supergiant and a post-RSG yellow supergiant/hypergiant about 10,763 light-years away in the constellation Carina. They are surrounded by a small circumstellar nebula, made of dust that was ejected in a recent high-mass-loss phase of the yellow supergiant
