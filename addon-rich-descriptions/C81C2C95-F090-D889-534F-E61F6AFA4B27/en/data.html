3 Juno is a large S-type asteroid in the asteroid belt. It was discovered by Karl Ludwig Harding in September 1804. Juno constitutes 1% of the total mass of the asteroid belt, and is about 254 kilometers in diameter.

NOTES:

  • This addon updates and modifies the existing version of asteroid Juno in Celestia.
  • Not to be confused with NASA's Juno mission sent to Jupiter.
