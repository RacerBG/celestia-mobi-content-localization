Spektr-RG is a Russian-German space observatory launched in 2019-Jul-13 into a halo orbit around the Sun-Earth Lagrange-2 point. It is designed to study the universe in x-ray light through its two primary instruments, eROSITA and ART-XC. It remains in active operation as of 2022
