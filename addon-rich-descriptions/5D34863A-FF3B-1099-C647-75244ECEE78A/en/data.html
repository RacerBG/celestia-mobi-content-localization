PDS 70, also known as V1032 Centauri, is a very young K-type T Tauri star about 369.7 light-years away in the constellation Centaurus. The star has a very dense protoplanetary disk with two super-Jovian planets orbiting it, the first one discovered is also the first protoplanet to be directly imaged
