The Orion III Spaceplane is an orbital spacecraft owned by Pan American World Airways and used in transit from Earth to Space Station V. This is the vehicle used by Dr. Heywood Floyd upon arriving at the station during the "Blue Danube" scene of 2001.

NOTES:

  • Download first the Space Station V addon in order for this addon to work
  • To see Orion III approaching the station, set your date to December 20, 1999 between 11:58 and 12:00 UTC
