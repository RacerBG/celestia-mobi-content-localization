Between 1975 and 1982, six soviet space probes landed on Venus. The Venera 9, 10, 11, 12, 13 and 14 landers provided the first images of the surface of the planet Venus. All these probes they didn't work for a long time. The record for operating time is held by Venera 13, which operated on the surface of Venus for 127 minutes.
