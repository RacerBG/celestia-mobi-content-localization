19P/Borrelly is a Jupiter-family comet that completes an orbit around the Sun once every 6.85 years. It is about 8 km in diameter, and was the fourth comet ever visited by a spacecraft when Deep Space 1 flew by its nucleus at a distance of 2,171 km in Sept 2001

NOTES:

  • This addon replaces the model and orbital parameters of the stock installation of Borrelly
  • The changes above allows the 2001-Sept-21 flyby of Deep Space 1 to be more accurate once both addons were installed
