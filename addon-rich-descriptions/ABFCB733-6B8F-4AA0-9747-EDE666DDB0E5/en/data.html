SiriusSat is a pair of Russian 1U-cubesats deployed from the ISS. Both satellites carry a particle detector designed to study space weather. It was launched Progress MS-09 on 2018-Jul-9, and were deployed from the station on 2018-Aug-15. They remain in low Earth orbit until 2020-Dec-9
