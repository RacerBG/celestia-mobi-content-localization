OGLE-2005-BLG-390Lb (nicknamed Hoth) is a super-Earth orbiting OGLE-2005-BLG-390L, a star 21,200 light-years from Earth near the center of the Milky Way, making it one of the most distant planets known. It is an icy exoplanet, far from the habitable zone of the star in which it orbits.
