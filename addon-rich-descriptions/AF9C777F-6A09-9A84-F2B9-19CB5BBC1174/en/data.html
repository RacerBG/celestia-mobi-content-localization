HR 8799 is a young A-type main-sequence star about 134.7 light-years away in the constellation Pegasus. Its system of four super-Jovians and a prominent debris disk is retroactively became the first star system whose orbital motion of its planets were directly imaged, after Fomalhaut b's existence was disproven in 2020
