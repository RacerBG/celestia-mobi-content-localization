A Rotovator is is a spinning tether designed to take craft from a planetary surface to escape velocity in one easy step. This one is around Mars. There are plenty of arranged shuttle encounters and a few hair raising near-collisions. Three orbiting hanging tethers are included as well. V2.0 (February, 2011): contains additional features.
