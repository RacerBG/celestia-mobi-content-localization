The first Death Star was the ultimate planet-destroying superweapon during the Imperial Era. A massive space station the size of a small moon (160 km), it is equipped with a powerful laser powered by kyber crystals, which the Empire tested to obliterate Jedha, Scarif, and later destroying Alderaan. It was destroyed by the Rebel Alliance before it manages to destroy their Base hidden on Yavin's fourth moon
