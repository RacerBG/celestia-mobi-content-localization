The supermassive black hole from the center of the Milky Way called Sagittarius A*

On May 12, 2022, the Event Horizon Telescope has publicly revealed the actual image of the supermassive black hole from the center of our own galaxy, the Milky Way! At around 27,000 light-years distant from Earth, it is the second such black hole imaged after P≈çwehi, the central black hole from Messier 87, in 2019.
